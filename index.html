Prompt Maker Utility for stable diffusion
Prompt:
Width
The width of the generated image.
Height
The height of the generated image.
Cfg Scale
Cfg scale adjusts how much the image will be like your prompt.
Higher values keep your image closer to your prompt.
Steps
How many steps to spend generating (diffusing) your image.
Number of Images
To generate multiple images from one prompt.
Seed
The seed used to generate your image.
Enable to manually set a seed.
